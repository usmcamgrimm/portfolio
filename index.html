Welcome!

My name is Adrian and I am a front-end developer passionate about writing HTML, CSS, and JavaScript that conforms to and exceeds established "best practices" of design and accessibility standards.

My open-source development journey began in 2013, building custom themes, frameworks(ROMs), and kernels for Android devices. Realizing I could turn my hobby into a career, I took classes in database administration. It wasn't until I took a JavaScript class though, that I found my passion for front-end development. I completed a certificate program in Web Design & Development, before discovering Vets Who Code, where I was able to develop a strong background in front-end skills while working with, and learning from, other veterans.

A Marine Corps veteran, I value teamwork and collaboration. My Marine Corps experiences also thrust me into the role of teacher, mentor, and learner. The combination of these roles has been instrumental to the beginning of my new career, because these roles taught me how to learn. Discipline is a key trait I have developed. Without discipline, I would not wake up early to write code before work, or spend time after work further developing my skills.

Plain and simple, I love writing code. I love the process, watching my creation as I bring an idea into fruition. I work on projects every day, and I've listed my current work in the projects area. If you like what you see, and would like to get to know me a little better, or see how well I'll fit with your company, please send me a message in my contact section below.
